HEY, I'M Ankitha!

I followed all the "right" steps to land my dream job in PR...only to get there and realize this wasn’t gonna fulfill me.

How could I have the spine-tingling, hair-raising, happy sigh-ing levels of freedom I desired while being stuck in the confines of a traditional job? I realized...I couldn’t.

I KNEW I NEEDED AN EXIT STRATEGY, SO I CREATED ONE FOR MYSELF

While my boss thought I was busy working on client projects, I secretly stowed away in my cubicle building my blog and Instagram.
